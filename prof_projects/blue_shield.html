Blue Shield of California

Competitive Analysis Insight Engine

Overview

This project involved developing a competitive analysis insight engine for Blue Shield of California, a leading health insurance provider. The goal was to create a tool that could analyze market data, identify trends, and generate actionable insights to inform strategic decision-making. Using a combination of data sources and advanced analytics techniques, the engine will be designed to provide a comprehensive view of the competitive landscape, helping Blue Shield stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry.

Exploratory Data Analysis

The first step in the project was to conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the Earning Call Transcripts from top Competitors. This involved cleaning and preprocessing the data, identifying key variables, and visualizing trends and patterns. Through EDA, we were able to uncover insights about market share, customer demographics, and competitive positioning, which laid the foundation for building the insight engine.
