San Francisco Chamber of Commerce (SFCC) Marketing Newsletters

Marketing Performance Analysis & Content Development

Overview

Contributed to the development and optimization of email newsletters for the San Francisco Chamber of Commerce, focused on member outreach, event promotion, and community engagement. Work spanned content creation, data-driven performance analysis, and cross-functional collaboration with multiple internal teams.

Rebranding & Content Development

Joined SFCC during a major rebranding effort and worked closely with the marketing team to ensure all newsletters reflected the new brand identity. This included maintaining consistency in tone, layout, and overall messaging across communications.

  • Aligned newsletters with updated branding guidelines
  • Collaborated with marketing to ensure consistency across all materials
  • Contributed to the transition toward a more cohesive brand presence

Newsletter Content Analysis & Optimization

Engaged with newsletter content and used engagement insights to guide improvements in layout and content prioritization.

  • Analyzed engagement patterns using heatmaps
  • Identified high-performing sections and drop-off points
  • Optimized content placement to increase visibility and interaction

Event Promotion & Strategy

Used heatmap data to understand where readers' attention was concentrated within each newsletter, then leveraged those insights to strategically place high-priority events in the highest-engagement zones. This ensured key events received maximum visibility rather than being buried in low-traffic sections.

  • Cross-referenced heatmap hotspots with event placement to determine optimal positioning
  • Highlighted high-priority events based on engagement data
  • Improved visibility of key initiatives through optimized placement
  • Tracked whether repositioned events saw measurable lift in click-through engagement

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Worked with multiple teams to support broader organizational goals, including promoting small businesses and internal initiatives such as Yes SF and the San Francisco Center for Economic Development.

  • Collaborated across teams to align messaging and outreach
  • Supported initiatives focused on local business growth and economic development
  • Helped increase awareness of SFCC programs through targeted communication
