Professional Background

I began my professional experience working at De Anza College as Technical Support. I then started my work in the public sector as a Fiscal Office Specialist with San Mateo County, where I handled financial transactions, maintained accurate records, and supported daily operations using tools like Excel. I then took my experience over to the San Francisco Chamber of Commerce (SFCC) working in their Marketing Team. At SFCC, I worked with various departments to promote the chamber's initiatives and support member businesses through an analytical lens.

Building on that foundation, I developed strong analytical and organizational skills while working in fast-paced, detail-oriented environments and collaborating across teams. More recently, I've applied these skills to data-focused projects, using tools like SQL and Power BI to analyze data, create dashboards, and turn insights into actionable recommendations on data projects for Blue Shield of California and the SEVA Foundation.
